Life at Our Church

Sunday Liturgy

Join us every Sunday at 7:00 AM for the Holy Qidase (Divine Liturgy) celebrated in Ge'ez and Amharic, following the ancient rites of the Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo tradition.

🤝

Community

Our congregation is a spiritual home for Ethiopian Orthodox families and a welcoming gathering place for anyone seeking to learn about our rich faith and cultural heritage.

📖

Roots of the Faith

The Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church traces its origins to the 4th century — one of the world's oldest Christian traditions, with a continuous, unbroken lineage of faith and Scripture.
